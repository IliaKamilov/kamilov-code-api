Data Deletion Instructions

1. Deleting Your Account and Data

To delete your account and personal data, please follow the steps below. By deleting your account, you will lose access to your personal data and services provided by us.

Step 1: Log in to Your Account

Go to your account login page and sign in with your credentials.

Step 2: Go to Account Settings

Once logged in, navigate to the "Account" or "Settings" page. Look for the section labeled "Account Deletion" or "Data Deletion."

Step 3: Request Account Deletion

Click on the "Delete Account" button and follow the on-screen instructions to proceed with account deletion.

Step 4: Contact Us (if no option is available)

If you cannot find the "Delete Account" option, please contact us directly at iliakmlv@gmail.com. Include your registered email address and request that your account and personal data be deleted.

2. What Happens When You Delete Your Account?

Once your account is deleted:

  • Your personal information, including name, email, and any stored preferences, will be permanently removed.
  • Any transactions, messages, or activity related to your account will be deleted.
  • You will no longer have access to our services or features tied to your account.

3. Deleting Specific Data Without Deleting the Account

If you prefer to delete specific data (e.g., personal information, stored preferences), you can do so by:

  • Visiting the "Privacy" or "Settings" section of your account.
  • Choosing options like "Delete Personal Data" or "Clear Search History." If no such option is available, please contact us for assistance.

4. Confirmation of Data Deletion

Once your request has been processed, we will send you a confirmation email notifying you that your data has been successfully deleted. If you have any further questions or issues, please contact our support team at iliakmlv@gmail.com.
